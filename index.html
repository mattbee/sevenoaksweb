Sevenoaks Web Scene

Welcome to Sevenoaks Web Scene, a monthly meetup for developers, designers and creatives in the Sevenoaks area. Meeting the first Tuesday of every month, follow on twitter for up to date news.

Join the SWS group on Get Together for updates and schedule

The next Sevenoaks Web Scene meetup will be on Tuesday 5th November 2019, at the Oak Tavern and Tap House, Sevenoaks High Street. Check gettogether.community for the latest details.

Slack Team

We have a Slack team for regular discussion between members, if you would like to be added, just send an email to matt@todayishould.com to request an invite.
